About Me

"Be Kind. For everyone you meet is fighting a battle you know nothing about." -- Unknown

"It's your road, and yours alone. Others may walk it with you, but no one can walk it for you." --Rumi

“For what it’s worth... it’s never too late, or in my case too early, to be whoever you want to be. There’s no time limit. Start whenever you want. You can change or stay the same. There are no rules to this thing. We can make the best or the worst of it. I hope you make the best of it. I hope you see things that startle you. I hope you feel things you’ve never felt before. I hope you meet people who have a different point of view. I hope you live a life you’re proud of, and if you’re not, I hope you have the courage to start over again.” -- F. Scott Fitzgerald

"Success is peace of mind which is a direct result of self-satisfaction in knowing you did your best to become the best you are capable of becoming." -- John Wooden


Hi there! These are one of my favorite quotes that resonate with me on how I'd like to view my pursuit of perpetual learning. Born and grew up in Tokyo most of my childhood, but I've also lived and/or visited 4 different continents around the world so far. My current "home" is Chicago with my dear husband who somehow tolerates my "random" spontaneous nature. We have two little munchkins (ages 1 and 3) who literally suck every bit of physical, mental and emotional energy left of us, but nevertheless fill our hearts in the way we could have never imagined. I love Chicago's summer and fall, but, my heart is always in San Francisco. I love to swim and I hope to always live by the water. I love Art History and fascinated by Statistics. Listening to elderly, or anyone really, talks about one's life story is truly music to my ears! My favorite cuisines are Peruvian and Moroccan. I believe sauteed mushrooms go well on absolutely anything--and of course, Shimeji mushrooms are the BEST. IMHO, more restaurants should serve soups before 11AM. And I'd love to learn how to skateboard, maybe in the near future...
